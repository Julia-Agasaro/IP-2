BIRTHDAY NAME GIVER

Introduction

This is a web application that takes
a user's birthday and calculates
the day of the week they were born
and then depending on their
gender outputs their Akan Name.

This is a very interesting Application
and is highly recommended feel free
to give it a try and find out
your Akan name
Let's hope you enjoy

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children
are given their first name
as a 'day name' which corresponds
to the day in the week they were born.
